About the Band

Masala Coffee, the dynamic band from Kerala, blends traditional South Indian melodies with contemporary sounds, creating a captivating musical fusion. With over a decade of performances worldwide, their vibrant compositions and eclectic lineup of talented musicians showcase their versatility and cultural richness. From soul-stirring performances to innovative compositions, Masala Coffee continues to charm audiences with their unique musical brew that celebrates Kerala's cultural heritage while embracing modern influences.

Masala Coffee made their mark with their debut album, 'Masala Coffee,' in 2014 which received widespread acclaim for its innovative sound and lyrical depth. Since then, the band has continued to enthrall audiences with their captivating compositions and soulful renditions.
